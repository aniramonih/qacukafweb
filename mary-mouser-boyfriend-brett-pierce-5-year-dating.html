Mary Mouser Boyfriend - Five Years and Counting for the Beautiful Couple

Mary Mouser is dating her boyfriend Brett Pierce, they have been together for over 5 years.
Image Source: Mary Mouser Instagram

Mary Mouser has been in show business since the age of six. Her first role was in 2002 and it has been almost two decades since her first outing. While not the same case, Mary's boyfriend, Brett Pierce, is also an actor, and no they did not meet while filming a movie or TV show.

The first meeting between the two happened on 11 November 2015 during a party they both were attending. Brett obviously liked Mary, little did he knew then, she liked him also. They got to talking soon after and started dating by the end of the month.

Even during their first meeting, Mary used a technique to see if he was into her. While talking during the party, she crossed her arms to see if he would mimic her actions, well, he did and the couple started a year-long dating process culminating in a proposal at Disneyland.

Mary Mouser Plays Samantha LaRusso in the Netflix Series Cobra Kai

Cobra Kai is the spinoff/sequel series branching from the original Karate Kid movie series. There were three movies and one spinoff film in the series and Cobra Kai is the continuation, after a few decades, from the last film in the trilogy.

Originally a YouTube Red production Netflix bought the rights to stream the series on their platform and later bought the future seasons outright to be shown exclusively on Netflix. The show was a success for YouTube Red but the streaming platform for YouTube has not worked as expected which is why the project was sold to Netflix.

Starring original Karate Kid Ralph Macchio, as Daniel LaRusso, and William Zabka as Johnny Lawrence, the story follows Johnny as a middle-aged man trying to earn some money teaching karate. The continuation of his rivalry with Daniel and the passing down of the Cobra Kai way, however flawed it may be.
