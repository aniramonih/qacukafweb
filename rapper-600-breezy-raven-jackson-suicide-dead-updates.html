Raven’s death announced by rapper

On September 6, 2022, Chicago-based rapper 600 Breezy posted a tribute to his girlfriend Raven K Jackson on social media, announcing her death.

She reportedly died by suicide, according to TMZ.

“I was coming back home I promised you I would…You just gone leave me forever?

Raven on TikTok

Raven K Jackson was an entrepreneur and influencer who shared fitness and mental health-focused content.

Before her untimely death, she also modeled and hosted her own mental health podcast.

Raven has more than 222,000 followers on her TikTok with 2.3million likes on her videos total.

She posted her last TikTok video on August 29 showing viewers how to recover an accidentally deleted Instagram reel to their page.

“Aww she’s so beautiful she looks kind of sad in her eyes,” someone commented on the video the day 600 Breezy announced that she had died on Instagram.

Chicago Rapper’s Girlfriend, continued

600 Breezy‘s third Instagram post about the death of his girlfriend, Raven K Jackson, were screenshots of different texts about the hardships of living that he said weren’t their last messages.

“THIS IS JUST ONE OF THE MANY TIMES LAST MONTH THAT I HAD TO STOP HER FROM,” he said in the caption of the post. “I JUST NEED YALL TO FEEL AND UNDERSTAND HER SO THIS CAN STOP HAPPENING.”

“IM [DYING] I WASN’T THERE FOR HER BUT YOU CAN BE THERE FOR YOUR LOVED ONES,” he added.

He said that Raven was hurting and that he did everything he could to keep her going.

“Please when your loved ones are showing signs of any mental damage or health please take them [seriously]. This is real,” he said.
