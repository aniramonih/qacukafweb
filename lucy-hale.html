Lucy Hale Biography

 Posted on August 15, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  365 words  |  Kelle Repass

Lucy was homeschooled and during her childhood, she took both singing and acting lessons. Lucy Hale started her career as a singer on the reality show "American Juniors" which she won in 2003. After that, Lucy moved to Los Angeles at the age of 15 to pursue her dream to be an actress.

Lucy started auditioning and received a minor role on "Drake & Josh" and other guest roles on shows such as "Ned's Declassified School Survival Guide", "The O.C.", and "How I Met Your Mother". She appeared in two episodes of the Disney Channel show Wizards of Waverly Place. Lucy has also worked as Aria Montgomery in the TV SERIES "Pretty Little Liars" which made her known in December 2009.
