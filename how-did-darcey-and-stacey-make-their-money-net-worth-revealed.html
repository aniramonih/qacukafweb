Twin sisters Darcey and Stacey, known for appearances on TLC’s 90 Day Fiance, have made quite the name for themselves in recent years.

The pair, who have been very open about their relationships and life dramas, are not been shy about how they’ve made their money, boasting a luxurious lifestyle on their social media.

However, before they ventured into the world of reality TV, the pair were already trying to make a name for themselves.

Reality Titbit has all the information you need to know about how they made the twin sisters made their fortune.

Darcey and Stacey’s net worth

Combined, the twin sisters have a net worth totalling around $3 million.

According to ScreenRant, Darcey’s net worth totals around $2 million as of 2021, leaving Stacey with an estimated $1 million.

Of all 90 Day Fiance stars across all seasons, Darcey is one of the cast members with the largest estimated net worth.

Darcey and Stacey: How they made money

Before Darcey and Stacey hit our screens, the identical sisters had many business ventures.

In 2010, at the age of 35, Darcey and Stacey co-founded their first business together which was a fashion brand called House of Eleven.

The brand, which still exists today, sells a range of items from jewellery to jackets, with items ranging from $8-$250.

The sisters also went on to produce the comedy movie, White T, in 2013 where they worked as co-executive producers to their father, Mike Silva.

The movie was produced by Darcey and Stacey’s own production company, Eleventh Entertainment, where they also produced a song in which they sang as background vocalists and was featured in White T.

The pair went on to pursue their singing career in 2018 with two singles, the first, ‘Lock Your Number’, which has over 22,000 streams on Spotify.

Darcey and Stacey: Reality TV career

Their first cameo on reality TV which made a name for the Silva sisters was on 90 Day Fiance where Darcey predominantly remained in the spotlight as a main cast member on the show.

Although TLC has not revealed the exact earnings that the show’s cast earns for their appearances, it has been reported that the American cast members are paid roughly $1,000 – $1,500 per episode.

As Darcey joined the show for over 4 seasons from 2017-2020, with a total of 51 episodes spanning those seasons, we can imagine that Darcey made a fair amount of money from the show.

With their popularity on the show, the sisters started their reality TV series in 2020 called ‘Darcey and Stacey’ which shares an insight into their personal lives.

Having so many views after its first season, the show did so well that it welcomed its second season on 19th July 2021.
