General Hospital Spoilers: Sasha Exposes Gladys Sabotage See How She Proves Cody Right?

 Posted on August 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  382 words  |  Martina Birk

General Hospital (GH) spoilers tease that Cody Bell (Josh Kelly) will keep hoping that Sasha Gilmore (Sofia Mattsson) will someday accept all the facts. Although Sasha no longer believes Cody stole the bracelet, she still doesn’t think Gladys Corbin (Bonnie Burroughs) was the one who set him up.

Of course, Cody is certain Gladys framed him and is counting on Sasha to see the light eventually.

That could happen in the very near future since Gladys is so desperate to stay in charge of Sasha’s finances.

At this point, Gladys knows having access to Sasha’s money is the only thing that can save her from the mountain of debt she’s under.

Selina Wu (Lydia Look) agreed to let Gladys pay her in installments, but the clock is still ticking since she expects to have all the necessary funds by the time she’s back from her Singapore trip.

Unfortunately, Selina also expects interest, which means Gladys will need even more time to sneakily transfer money over to Ms. Wu.

That’s why Gladys needs the guardianship to be extended instead of cut short.

Sasha mentioned that she was going to meet with her doctor and discuss getting the guardianship lifted, so GH spoilers say this could be Gladys’ last chance to intervene.

Will Gladys mislead the doctor about Sasha’s behavior and try to act like she’s not ready to handle her bank account on her own?

Gladys might even resort to getting Sasha riled in front of the doctor so that she looks unstable.

It’s clear that Gladys is scrambling and willing to take drastic measures, so another betrayal may be brewing.

If nothing else, Sasha could overhear Gladys’ attempt to undermine her with the doc and get this guardianship extended.

It could finally tip Sasha off to the fact that Gladys doesn’t have her best interests at heart anymore.

Will Sasha get updates on Gladys’ debt and how she’s been exploiting this guardianship situation? Will Sasha expose Gladys’ sabotage and decide Cody was right all along?

General Hospital spoilers say Sasha will turn against her mother-in-law sooner or later, so Gladys had better be ready for terrible news and major backlash.
