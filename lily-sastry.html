Lily Ditched her fathers' surname and replaced with her mothers 

The whole world was in a dilemma about what they heard when they heard, Rowan Atkinson, aka Mr. Bean, was separating from his wife. Lily rose to fame with the last name of her father. i.e., Atkinson.
