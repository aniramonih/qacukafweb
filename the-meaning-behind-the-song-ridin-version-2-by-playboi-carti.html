The Meaning Behind The Song: Ridin (Version 2) by Playboi Carti

Writer/Composer:Playboi Carti
Album:Unreleased
Release Date:January 5, 2023
Genre:Rap, Cloud Rap, Hip-Hop, Atlanta Rap, Trap

“Ridin’ (Version 2)” is an unreleased song by Playboi Carti that leaked on January 5, 2023. This is the second version of “Ridin’ (Born Sinner)” and it features an alternate verse. The song is produced by KP Beatz and Fluxury.

As a fan of Playboi Carti, I have always been intrigued by the meaning behind his songs. “Ridin’ (Version 2)” is no exception. While the lyrics may seem straightforward and aggressive at first glance, there is a deeper message that can be interpreted from the song.

The chorus of the song starts with the line “I already took my shirt off, hold up, yeah.” This line could symbolize vulnerability and a willingness to expose oneself emotionally. The act of taking off one’s shirt could represent shedding layers and being authentic. The repeated plea to the Lord for a break and spareness could be interpreted as a cry for mercy and a desire for relief from life’s struggles.

Personally, I relate to this song on a deep level. There have been times in my life when I have felt overwhelmed and in need of a break. The relentless pursuit of success and the pressures of everyday life can take a toll on one’s mental and emotional well-being. “Ridin’ (Version 2)” captures the raw emotions and desperation that one can feel when faced with these challenges.

The verses in the song depict a sense of recklessness and a disregard for consequences. The mention of being tatted all over the body and the devil watching closely could represent a rebellion against societal norms and a willingness to embrace a darker side. The references to drugs and killers behind the protagonist add to the gritty and dangerous atmosphere of the song.

However, amidst the aggression and intensity, there is a glimmer of hope. The line “Made it out the mud, yeah, now we having fun” suggests that despite the hardships and struggles, there is still joy and enjoyment to be found in life. The abundance of money and a carefree attitude towards others may be seen as a coping mechanism or a way of finding temporary relief from the weight of existence.

Overall, “Ridin’ (Version 2)” by Playboi Carti is a powerful and introspective song that delves into the complexities of human emotions. It explores themes of vulnerability, desperation, and the constant pursuit of temporary happiness. While the lyrics may be interpreted differently by each individual listener, the underlying message remains the same: the desire for a break and a plea for mercy in the face of life’s challenges.
