Musiala is a flexible midfielder who can play in all areas of the midfield in addition to the wings. League Career:

Musiala moved to Bundesliga side Bayern Munich from Chelsea in July 2019. In Bayern Munich II’s 3-2 victory over Preußen Münster, Musiala made his professional debut by substituting for the team. As the youngest player to represent Bayern in a Bundesliga game, he made his league debut against Freiburg.

Musiala became Bayern’s youngest goalscorer when he scored his first Bundesliga goal in a victory over Schalke, beating the previous mark held by Roque Santa Cruz. Musiala replaced Thomas Müller in the Champions League for the first time. Musiala became the youngest English and German goal scorer in the Champions League when he scored his first goal against Lazio in the round of 16 matches. His first professional deal was till 2026 with Bayern Munich.

International Career:

Musiala declared that he had chosen to compete for his native Germany in all international competitions. Later, in March 2021, he received his first call-up to the senior team for the 2022 FIFA World Cup qualifiers. He made his debut on March 25, 2021, against Iceland as a 79th-minute substitute in a 3-0 victory, and he scored his first goal on October 11, 2021, in a 4-0 victory over North Macedonia. He was selected for the team for the UEFA Euro 2020 on May 19, 2021. At the age of 18 years, he played in a 2-2 tie with Hungary during Euro 2020 to become the youngest German player to appear in a major tournament.
