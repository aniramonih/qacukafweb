Michele Gumabao, born on September 2, 1992, is a talented Filipino-American who has made her mark as a model, volleyball player, and TV personality. She gained immense popularity when she participated in the Miss Universe Philippines 2020 contest, where she secured the honorable title of the second runner-up.

Early Life and Family

  • Michele Gumabao was born in San Mateo, California, United States.
  • She is the daughter of Dennis Roldan, a former PBA player and actor, and Loli Imperial-Gumabao.
  • She grew up with five siblings: Marco, Margarita, Marielle, Mitchell, and Katrina.

 

Career Highlights

  • Michele’s rise to stardom began when she competed in the Miss Universe Philippines 2020 pageant.
  • This contest was a significant departure from the traditional Binibining Pilipinas Charities, Inc., making it the first version of Miss Universe Philippines.
  • Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the crowning night was delayed to June 14, 2020, and the final contest took place on October 25, 2020.
  • Michele Gumabao impressed the audience and judges, earning the title of the second runner-up in the Miss Universe Philippines 2020.

Personal Life and Relationships

  • Michele Gumabao is currently in a relationship with Aldo Panlilio, a Filipino basketball player.

Education and Career in Sports

  • Michele attended the School of the Holy Spirit in Quezon City during her childhood.
  • She later pursued higher education at De La Salle University, where she graduated with a degree in Bachelor of Science with a major in marketing management.
  • Michele’s love for sports led her to become a prominent member of the De La Salle University women’s volleyball team, known as the Lady Spikers.
  • During her time in college, she contributed to her team’s success, winning three consecutive championship titles in the UAAP.

Modeling and TV Career

  • Besides her passion for sports, Michele Gumabao ventured into the world of modeling and TV.
  • She began her modeling journey by participating in Binibining Pilipinas in 2018, showcasing her versatility and beauty.
  • Michele represented the Philippines in the Miss Globe beauty pageant in 2018, adding to her impressive portfolio.
  • Her talent and charm caught the attention of renowned brands like Nike, Calvin Klein, Dior, Adidas, Gucci, and Chanel, with whom she collaborated.
