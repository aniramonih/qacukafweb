What did Camila Mendes wear at Met Gala 2022?

Camila Mendes made a stunning appearance at the 2022 Met Gala in a dress that paid homage to the iconic Electric Light Dress by Alice Claypoole Vanderbilt at the Vanderbilt costume ball in 1883.

The theme of the Met Gala was “In America: An Anthology of Fashion,” and Mendes took the “Gilded Glamour” theme seriously with her golden tinsel dress. The dress, designed by Ami, featured intricate metallic thread embroidery and yellow tulle with applied tinsel, mimicking the shimmering effect of Vanderbilt’s Electric Light Dress.

Mendes accessorized the look with Fred Leighton jewels, including 19th-century citrine and gold pendant earrings and a 1940s citrine and gold ring. Her makeup was also on point, with a glowy look that channeled the lit-up effect of Vanderbilt’s Electric Light Dress.

The overall effect was a modern take on a classic look that showcased Mendes’s impeccable fashion sense and style. This was not Mendes’s first time at the Met Gala, having made her debut in 2019 wearing a yellow Prabal Gurung dress.

Camila Mendes debut Met Gala 2019 Explored

Camila Mendes debuted at the 2019 Met Gala. The Riverdale star arrived with her co-star and ex-boyfriend, Charles Melton, and they both looked stunning on the red carpet.

Camila wore a beautiful yellow gown by Prabal Gurung, which featured a yellow bodice that turned into a stream of yellow and white feathers to the ground. The dress had a large bow train, and Camila carried a hard-case clutch with the word “Classy” written on it.

She also wore jewelry from David Yurman and bright red lipstick. Charles Melton also looked dashing in a shimmery black-and-blue suit that caught all the light that hit him.

The couple showed off their love for each other as they posed for photos together, and fans couldn’t get enough of their adorable chemistry.

In an interview with W Magazine, Camila revealed that she had tried on the dress for the first time on Saturday night, which was effortless.

She also said there was a good amount of preparation and open-mindedness to try new things for the event. Overall, Mendes’ debut at the 2019 Met Gala was a huge success.
