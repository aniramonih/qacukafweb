Age, Birthday & Biography

She was arrested after a video of the incident went viral on social media platforms, where she was seen shouting at and hitting the students, and asking them to get down from the bus. She was later released on bail after a few hours. She defended her actions and said that she was trying to save the lives of the students who were risking their lives by traveling on the footboard and rooftop of the bus. She also alleged that the police had harassed her and that the male police officers had banged on the windows of her house while she was changing her clothes inside.

She received mixed reactions from the public and the media for her actions. Some people supported her and praised her for taking a stand against the dangerous practice of footboard travel, while others criticized her and condemned her for assaulting and abusing the students and government employees.
