Julian Wiggins, of San Diego, Fulfillment Specialist &amp; Senior X Recruiter, passes away: Obituary

 Posted on August 26, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  358 words  |  Tandra Barner

San Diego, CA – The San Diego County community and Aya Healthcare family are mourning the unexpected passing of Julian Wiggins, a devoted Fulfillment Specialist and Senior X Recruiter at Aya Healthcare in Santee, California. Julian’s sudden demise has left his family, friends, and colleagues devastated.

Julian Wiggins was more than just a colleague; he was a dedicated and cheerful worker whose impact in the healthcare industry resonates with everyone fortunate enough to have crossed paths with him. A member of the Phi Gamma Delta Fraternity, Julian was known for his strong work ethic and genuine care for his family, the future, and his community.

During his tenure at Aya Healthcare, Julian’s contributions were invaluable. His roles encompassed directing branch sales, operations, fleet planning, marketing, and production. He mentored numerous employees, guiding them to the Assistant Branch Manager position. Julian’s innovative approaches were a driving force behind the successful implementation of new sales and upgrade pitches, leading to a remarkable increase in sales and revenue, often surpassing 30% growth every month and accounting for over $100,000 in additional operating profit compared to the previous year.

Julian Wiggins was exceptional in his teamwork efficiency and demonstrated outstanding leadership skills. He supervised two brands, overseeing a team of 60+ employees and managing a fleet of +7000 vehicles, generating an average revenue of over $1 million per month. His influence was particularly significant, elevating his branch to the number 1 position at the airport in North America. Under his guidance, customer service and sales substantially improved, achieving all the set goals for the first time in over a year. He was instrumental in augmenting sales numbers by $100,000 and service by 10%. Julian also introduced advanced training methods to address areas of opportunity.

The cause of Julian Wiggins’ untimely passing has not been released by the family at this time. As the community mourns this loss, they remember Julian for his dedication, exceptional work, and the positive impact he made in the lives of those around him. He will be dearly missed, and his legacy will forever remain in the hearts of all who had the privilege of knowing him.
