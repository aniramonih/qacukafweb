My Christmas Inn Filming Locations

Despite the fact that the story takes place in Alaska, the movie was shot in Salt Lake City, which is located in the state of Utah. The production crew was able to successfully recreate rural Alaskan landscapes because to Utah’s extensive natural diversity, which provided the perfect backdrops for their work.

Salt Lake City, Utah

The city of Salt Lake City in Utah proved to be an excellent choice for the shooting of “My Christmas Inn.” You won’t have to travel far to get to the Great Salt Lake from the state capital of Utah, which is situated in the midst of a magnificent mountain range that provides some stunning vistas.

The modest Alaskan village from the movie was recreated using both outdoor and indoor settings in Salt Lake City, which served as the setting for the filming of the scene.

The sets of the movies were decked out in holiday decorations to give the idea that it was a joyful time of year, and fake snow was employed to give the sense that winter had arrived.

Other Places In Utah

In addition to filming in Salt Lake City, the cast and crew also shot a number of sequences in Provo and other areas of Utah, as well as in other cities and towns across the state.
