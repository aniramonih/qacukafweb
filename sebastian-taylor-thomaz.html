Who is Sebastian Taylor Thomaz?

Sebastian Taylor Thomaz, also known as “The Bash,” is a highly recognized celebrity child in the United States. Born on February 21, 2013, he is the son of the award-winning rapper Wiz Khalifa and Amber Rose, a renowned American model.

Despite his young age, Sebastian has already garnered quite a bit of attention from the media due to his famous parents. He often accompanies them at various events, including award shows, fashion shows, and other public appearances.

Although Sebastian’s parents are in the public eye, they have made a concerted effort to shield their son from the spotlight as much as possible. They have expressed their desire to allow Sebastian a normal childhood and grow away from the paparazzi’s prying eyes.

Despite his parents’ efforts to keep him out of the spotlight, Sebastian has become a beloved celebrity figure, with many admiring his adorable personality and undeniable charm. As he grows up, seeing what the future holds for this young celebrity will be exciting.

Quick Facts

Sebastian Taylor Thomaz Birthday

Sebastian Taylor Thomaz was born on February 21, 2013. He was born in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, USA. As of 2023, he is 10 years old.

Parents

Wiz Khalifa (Father)

Wiz Khalifa is an American rapper famous for his single “Black and Yellow.” Born Cameron Jibril Thomaz, Wiz Khalifa has also worked as a songwriter and actor. 

In 2006, Wiz Khalifa released his debut album, Show and Prove, after which he signed to Warner Bros. Records the following year. Although his single “Say Yeah” was a minor hit charting on the Rhythmic Top 40 and Hot Rap Tracks Charts in 2008, his debut single for Atlantic Records (signed in April 2010), “Black and Yellow,” helped the rapper catapult to fame.

“Black and Yellow” debuted at number 100 and peaked at number 1 on the Billboard Hot 100. In March 2011, Wiz Khalifa released his third album Rolling Papers, and included “Black and Yellow” as the lead single along with other Top 40 singles such as “Roll Up” and “No Sleep.”

In December 2012, he released his fourth album ONIFC which included the singles “Work Hard, Play Hard” and “Remember You.” His fifth album Blacc Hollywood was released in August 2014. 

Wiz Khalifa collaborated with Charlie Puth for “See You Again,” the soundtrack of Furious 7, which remained on the Billboard Hot 100 for 12 non-consecutive weeks. The rapper has been honored with many awards, including the BET Award, Billboard Music Awards, ASCAP Pop Music Awards, BET Hip Hop Awards, and Nickelodeon Kids’ Choice Awards. 

Amber Rose (Mother)

Amber Rose is an American model who rose to fame following her high-profile two-year-long romantic relationship with rapper Kanye West. Also a rapper and television personality, Amber Rose starred in the music video for Young Jeezy and Kanye West’s single “Put On.”

Featured in several of Kanye West’s ads with Louis Vuitton, Amber Rose signed a modeling contract with Ford Models in 2009. In 2010, Rose and West ended their relationship. 

After their breakup, Amber Rose dated and tied the knot with rapper Wiz Khalifa. The pair divorced in 2014. 

Amber Rose has also authored a book called How to Be a Bad Bitch. In addition, she founded the Los Angeles chapter of the SlutWalk protest march.

In 2016, Rose’s talk show titled The Amber Rose Show started airing on VH1. Her syndicated radio call-in program Loveline also debuted the same year.

Amber Rose has released and collaborated on several songs, including “Fame,” “Loaded,” and “Rise Above.” In addition, Rose participated in the 23rd season of Dancing with the Stars.

Wiz Khalifa and Amber Rose Relationship

Wiz Khalifa and Amber Rose began dating in 2011. The couple enjoyed a public romance and even appeared together in a spread for FADER magazine. 

In March 2011, making a joint appearance on MTV’s “RapFix Live,” Wiz Khalifa commented on their relationship, saying,

“We just stay positive with each other; that’s the important thing. The game will try to tear you down and make you feel like what you already have is not enough. We’re very, very close, and we understand each other’s life.”

In 2012, Amber Rose made headlines after getting a tattoo of Wiz Khalifa on her arm. The pair made their red carpet debut during the 2011 Video Music Awards (VMA) and also revealed that they were “very much in love” and were taking “our time and just enjoy each other right now” before tying the knot.

In March 2012, Wiz proposed to Amber with a stunning oval-cut diamond ring. The rapper shared the happy news with his fans via Twitter and wrote, “She Said Yes!!!” 

In September 2012, Amber announced her pregnancy on the 2012 VMA red carpet. Their son, Sebastian Taylor Thomaz, was born on February 21, 2013. 

Wiz tweeted Sebastian’s arrival through a tweet that read:

“Happy Birthday Sebastian ‘The Bash’ Taylor Thomaz!!! Everyone, welcome this perfect young man into the world.”

Amber marked Wiz’s 27th birthday and posted their wedding dance as a tribute to her husband. She captioned the video,

“Happy 27th Birthday @mistercap I’ll love you forever…. Hope this year is an amazing one for you, sweetheart. ❤️.”

However, just two weeks later, news started doing the rounds that Amber Rose had filed for divorce. After news about the couple’s separation broke, TMZ News revealed that Wiz had cheated on Amber. 

A source close to the couple shared with TMZ that Wiz “didn’t want to be married to her anymore.” Peter Rosenberg of New York’s Hot 97 weighed in on Wiz Khalifa and Amber Rose’s split, revealing that Rose had walked in on Wiz having a threesome with twin sisters. The former couple shares joint custody of their son, Sebastian Taylor Thomaz. 

Siblings

Sebastian Taylor Thomaz is the only son of rapper Wiz Khalifa and model Amber Rose. However, he has a half-brother. Sebastian’s mom Amber Rose welcomed her son, Slash, with Jam record label executive Alexander Edwards in October 2019.
