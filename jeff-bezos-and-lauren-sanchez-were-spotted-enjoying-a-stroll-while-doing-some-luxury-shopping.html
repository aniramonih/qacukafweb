Jeff Bezos and GF Lauren Sanchez exude winter glamor as they walk hand-in-hand while luxury shopping

 Posted on August 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  408 words  |  Patria Henriques

ASPEN, COLORADO: Jeff Bezos and Lauren Sanchez looked stylish as they were seen strolling in the winter town of Aspen together. The 58-year-old former Amazon CEO and the 53-year-old media personality held hands as they walked past the Louis Vuitton store in Colorado, on Wednesday, December 28. The Amazon billionaire wore a cream-colored jacket and a gray cap, while the Black Ops Aviation founder wore a hot pink '80s-style coat tied at the waist with a belt. A group of friends joined Jeff and Lauren at the luxury retail store.

Lauren complemented her chic winter outfit with a white turtleneck sweater, light gray leggings and white Chanel boots. A pair of aviator glasses and a brown Hermes handbag completed her outfit. Bezos himself opted for brown tinted sunglasses. For her romantic walk, the American entrepreneur wore black lace-up boots.

Later that day, the two visited the Kemo Sabe store in Aspen to do their shopping. While customers pulled out their cell phones to take photos, the two left the store wearing cowboy hats.

At the beginning of her career, Sanchez worked as a news anchor and reporter for Fox 11's 'Good Day LA' and as a journalist for 'Extra.' Her most recent career was as a helicopter pilot, and in 2016, she established Black Ops Aviation, her own aerial filmmaking business. According to her IMDB biography, Sanchez has also had roles in both television and movies, including hosting the reality series 'So You Think You Can Dance' and portraying an anchor in films like 'Fight Club' and 'The Day After Tomorrow.'

Last month, the couple revealed in their very first interview together that their average Saturday night consists of dinner and a movie with their kids. The two have a total of seven children together from previous relationships. Bezos, the fourth richest person in the world, and Sanchez said that being 'teammates' strengthens their relationship. In 2023, Sanchez will become the first woman to launch into space, just three years after Bezos.

Bezos was previously married to MacKenzie Scott in 1993 and divorced after 25 years of marriage on January 9, 2019. The couple has four children together. While Lauren was married to Patrick Whitesell who finalized their divorce on October 2019 and they share two kids together.
