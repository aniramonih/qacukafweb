Early Life & Biography

Amaury Nolasco, whose full name is Amaury Nolasco Garrido, was born on the 24th of December 1970 in Puerto Rico to parents, Camelia Garrido and Ángel Nolasco. He didn’t want to become an actor initially and had even studied Biology at the University of Puerto Rico.  However, he changed his mind and began taking acting gigs. After various acting gigs, he moved to New York City and began training at the American British Dramatic Arts School. He has also attended The Marjorie Ballentine Studio.
