Rod Christopher Godwin Jr., an American football wide receiver with the National Football League’s Tampa Bay Buccaneers, was born on February 27, 1996. (NFL).

Godwin was selected by the Buccaneers in the third round of the 2017 NFL Draft while playing college football for Penn State.

In Middletown, Delaware, Godwin attended Middletown High School. Godwin was an integral part of the Cavaliers’ 41-7 record and two State Championships during his four years as a high school football player.

Every year of his high school career, Godwin had more than 700 yards, and as a senior, he had 1,150 yards and 16 receiving touchdowns.

In high school, Godwin received awards such as Delaware Gatorade Player of the Year, selection to play in the 2013 Under Armour All-American Game, All-state honors as a sophomore, junior, and senior, four-time All-Conference wide receiver, and kick returner, among others.
