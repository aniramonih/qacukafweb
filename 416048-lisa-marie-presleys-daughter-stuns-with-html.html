Lisa-Marie Presley's Daughter Stuns with Dark Brown Hair: 'Looks So Much like Her Mom, OMG'

 Posted on August 29, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  653 words  |  Martina Birk
  • Riley Keough, a phenomenal actress, is known for having bold hair colors such as red.
  • The daughter of Lisa Marie Presley recently debuted a new hair color.
  • Fans loved Keough's new hair shade because it made her look more like her mom.

Lisa Marie Presley and her daughter Riley Keough at 20th Annual Night of Stars Awards Gala in New York City on October 30, 2003 | Source: Getty Images

Riley Keough is an actress who has featured in productions such as "The Lodge," "Mad Max: Fury Road," "Under the Silver Lake," and "Logan Lucky." Her feature film debut was in "The Runaways," in a supporting role portraying Marie Currie.

Riley Keough at the 2023 Vanity Fair Oscar Party in Beverly Hills, California on March 12, 2023 | Source: Getty Images

The actress is known for her signature colorful hair look, which she changes from time to time. Keough recently debuted a new hair color, which fans say makes her resemble her mother, Lisa Marie Presley, who was also an actress.

Keough has, in the past, opened up about her hair care. The granddaughter of Elvis Presley hates haircuts and avoids makeup. She often has rockstar hair, with Tracey Cunningham doing her colors.

Riley Keough at Paris Fashion Week in Paris, France on July 04, 2023 | Source: Getty Images

Since Keough doesn't like cutting her hair, Stacey Ho, who works in Cunningham's salon, forces her to cut it. Still, the actress doesn't believe that cutting hair will make it grow longer. She said her secret to long hair is never to cut it, air drying it, and putting some kind of moisturizer in it.

Other fans pointed out how "beautiful" the fresh hair shade looked on Keough.

Keough, who feels that dry hair is wild and crazy, once called her hair color "accidental ombre." She previously had red hair, brown, auburn, and even blonde. Aside from changing her hair color, the actress is super natural with her hair and mostly buys the products she uses on it at Whole Foods.

The 34-year-old once showed her only picture with natural hair color on Instagram. Keough is usually a brunette, and in the picture, her hair is slightly dark with wild curly waves. The actress recently debuted another hair color, from her previous red hair, as she attended an event.

Riley Keough, wearing CHANEL, at the Academy Women's Luncheon Presented By CHANEL in Los Angeles, California on November 09, 2023. | Source: Getty Images

Keough's New Hair Color and Fans' Comments

Keough showed off her new hair shade, dark brown, on November 9, 2023, at the Academy's Women Luncheon, an annual celebration of women in the industry. The event took place just hours after the end of the 118-day actors' strike.

The event shines light on the Academy's Gold Fellowship for Women' and was attended by A-list actors such as Greta Lee, Lupita Nyong'o, and America Ferrera. Keough was at the event too, with her dark brown hair, done by Gregory Russell, a Los Angeles-based hairstylist, catching the attention of many.

Riley Keough, wearing CHANEL, at the Academy Women's Luncheon Presented By CHANEL in Los Angeles, California on November 09, 2023. | Source: Getty Images

Keough's fans saw pictures of her new hair color online and commented on it. They said the hair shade made her look "even more like Lisa Marie." "She looks so much like her mom omg," a fan agreed.

Keough's fans love just how her new hair shade makes her resemble her mom more than her other hair colors. The actress is a lover of bold colors when it comes to hair. She has now chosen to have a dark brown color on her hair that she cherishes and takes good care of.
