How Old Are Bluey and Bingo and Where Do They Live?

 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  622 words  |  Martina Birk

Bluey is 6 years old while Bingo, her little sister, is 4 years old. Bluey and Bingo are both young Heeler puppies and they live with their parents in the fictional Australian town of Brisvegas.

The eponymous Kids’ animated television series, premiered in Australia in 2018. The show was created by Joe Brumm and produced in Brisbane, Australia by Ludo Studio in collaboration with the Australian Broadcasting Corporation (ABC) and the British Broadcasting Corporation (BBC). It quickly gained popularity for its relatable stories, endearing characters, and high-quality animation.

The series revolves around the adventures of Bluey, a six-year-old Blue Heeler puppy, and her family. Bluey lives with her dad, Bandit; her mom, Chilli; and her younger sister, Bingo. Together, they navigate everyday life, engaging in imaginative play, exploring their surroundings, and learning important life lessons along the way.

How Old Is Bluey?

Bluey is a lively and sociable six-year-old blue heeler puppy. She has a knack for creating imaginative games and enjoys guiding others in choosing their roles and establishing rules. Bluey particularly loves games that involve multiple children and adults, with her father being a favorite playmate. She often immerses herself in grown-up scenarios, taking charge and instructing everyone on what to do.

How Old Is Bingo From Bluey?

Bingo from Bluey is 4 years old. She is a red heeler like her mother and possesses distinct qualities that set her apart from her sister, Bluey. Unlike Bluey, Bingo is quieter and more reserved, displaying a mix of readiness and timidity when it comes to play. At times, she struggles to be courageous and express her emotions openly. Despite this, Bingo holds a deep admiration for her sister and cherishes the games they engage in together. One particular delight for her is when Bluey invites her to assume the role of a doctor rather than the patient.

Apart from her bond with Bluey, Bingo finds joy in conversing with tiny bugs in the yard and often immerses herself in her own enchanting world of imagination.

Where Does Bluey Live?

Bluey lives in a fictional Australian town called “Brisvegas.” The town is specifically created for the show and serves as the setting for Bluey’s adventures with her family. While Brisvegas is not an actual town or city in Australia, it is presented as a representation of a typical Australian suburban area. The show’s creators aimed to capture the essence of Australian culture, lifestyle, and values through the depiction of Bluey’s home and surroundings.

Throughout the series, viewers can see elements of Australian life reflected in the settings and activities of Bluey and her family. The show often showcases iconic Australian backyards, parks, beaches, and natural environments. The animation style incorporates distinct Australian flora and fauna, such as gum trees, kangaroos, and kookaburras, further emphasizing the Australian setting.

Where is Bluey From? Is She Australian?

Bluey, the animated character, is from the fictional town of Brisvegas in Australia, making her an Australian. In the Bluey television series, which is set in Australia, Brisvegas serves as the home for Bluey and her family. However, it’s important to note as previously mentioned, that Brisvegas is not an actual town or city in Australia. It is a playful and fictional representation of a typical Australian suburban area created specifically for the show.

In addition to the physical surroundings, the show also incorporates Australian slang, phrases, and cultural references into the dialogue. This adds an extra layer of authenticity and familiarity for Australian audiences, while also introducing international viewers to unique aspects of Australian language and expressions.
