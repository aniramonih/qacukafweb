Breakthrough with Wild ‘n Out and Other Projects

DC Young Fly’s big break came in 2015 when he joined the cast of Wild ‘n Out, a comedy event with rap battles, improv games, and celebrity guests. He made his cinematic debut the following year in the comedy film Almost Christmas, featuring Danny Glover, Mo’Nique, and Gabrielle Union.
