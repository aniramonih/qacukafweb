The Meaning Behind The Song: 900 Cigarros by La Fiera de Ojinaga

I recently came across a captivating song called “900 Cigarros” by La Fiera de Ojinaga. At first, I was drawn to the infectious melody and lively rhythm, but upon delving deeper into the lyrics, I discovered a poignant story of love and longing.

The song begins with the lines “Siempre que tomo, me da por llamarte, A como dé lugar, tu voz quiero escuchar” (Translation: “Whenever I drink, I feel the urge to call you, I want to hear your voice at any cost”). These opening lines set the tone for the entire song, expressing the protagonist’s yearning to connect with their loved one.

As the verse unfolds, the lyrics depict the narrator’s emotional journey, marked by heartbreak and nostalgia. “De tanto que he llorado, ya hice un diluvio, Y ya mero es Junio” (Translation: “I’ve cried so much that I’ve created a flood, and June is almost here”). These lines allude to the depth of their sorrow and the passage of time, emphasizing the intensity of their longing.

The chorus of the song paints a vivid picture of the memories and experiences shared with the loved one. “En estas fechas estaba enpiernado contigo, Tus besos en el cuello lo hacían todo más lindo” (Translation: “During this time, I was entangled with you, Your kisses on my neck made everything more beautiful”). The protagonist’s lament for these lost moments is palpable, especially when they express their desire to have their loved one back in their life. “¿Por qué no vuelves? Extraño hacertelo en el carro, Te llevo esperando 900 cigarros, Las estrellas no se miran igual, si no estoy en tus brazos” (Translation: “Why don’t you come back? I miss doing it with you in the car, I have been waiting for you for 900 cigarettes, The stars don’t look the same if I’m not in your arms”). These lyrics showcase a yearning for emotional and physical intimacy, and the absence of their loved one’s presence leaves a void in their life.

La Fiera de Ojinaga’s “900 Cigarros” beautifully captures the complexity of human emotions and the universal experience of longing for lost love. This song resonates deeply with me, as I have also experienced the bittersweet feeling of missing someone who used to share precious moments with me.

The song’s melody perfectly complements the lyrics, evoking a sense of nostalgia and melancholy. The choice of country genre adds a timeless quality to the song, enabling listeners to connect with its emotional core regardless of their cultural background.

As I listen to “900 Cigarros,” I am transported to a place where the memories and emotions of the past are intertwined. It serves as a reminder of the power that music holds to heal and provide solace during challenging times. The song encourages me to reflect on my own experiences and cherish the moments I have shared with loved ones.

In conclusion, “900 Cigarros” by La Fiera de Ojinaga is a heartfelt song that speaks to the universal theme of longing and loss. Its captivating lyrics and soul-stirring melody combine to create a powerful and emotional experience for listeners. This song reminds me of the profound impact that music can have on our lives, serving as a conduit for our deepest emotions and memories.
