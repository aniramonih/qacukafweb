Drake Bell is an American actor, voice actor, composer, and musician, best known for starring in the hit Nickelodeon sitcom, Drake and Josh. Bell won nine Nickelodeon Kid’s Choice Awards for the show. He also co-wrote and performed the theme song for the Drake & Josh Show Found a Way

Originally, Bell gained national recognition as a child star in another Nickelodeon comedy, The Amanda Show which ran from the late 90s to the early 2000s. Drake Bell was a judge on the Miss Teen USA 2006.

After filing for bankruptcy a few years ago, Bell turned to his music in a bid to change his fortunes and rekindle his career. He’s been busy promoting himself on radio stations across the country, meeting Drake and Josh fans, and promoting the Drake Bell Entertainment YouTube channel.

Bio, Age

Born Jared Drake Bell on June 27, 1986 in SantaAna, California to Robin and Joe Bell, Drake is of English and Scandinavian descent. His mother, Robin Dodson, was a professional pool player. He has three older brothers, Joey, Rob and Travis and an older sister, Kellie. Her parents divorced and in 1995 her mother remarried her current husband Roy. This added a number of half-siblings on his mother’s and father’s side. He attended the prestigious Orange County High School of the Arts in Santa Ana.

Drake Bell made his first TV appearance in a small commercial for Whirlpool Appliances when he was just 5 years old. He has also done commercials for Pepperidge Farm Goldfish Crackers and Fruit Gushers. He debuted in a 1994 episode of Home Improvement. He has since proven himself in both arenas of comedy and pop recording.

Bell has his own Drake Bell group, originally called Drake 24/7. He also owns a record label, Backhouse Records, and is the face of The Thirst Project – a Los Angeles-based nonprofit that helps raise awareness and educate people about the clean water crisis.

Drake Bell’s career hasn’t been all rosy as he’s had his fair share of bad luck. In the waning times of 2005, the actor was involved in a car accident that left him with a broken jaw, fractured vertebrae, broken teeth and severe facial lacerations. Worse still, he lost his ability to play guitar due to an extreme wrist injury.
