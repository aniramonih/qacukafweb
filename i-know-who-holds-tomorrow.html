Overall Meaning

The song "I Know Who Holds Tomorrow" by Barbara Jones is a powerful hymn that speaks to a deep trust in God's plan for our lives, even in the face of uncertainty and adversity. The opening lyrics acknowledge the fact that we cannot predict what tomorrow will bring, but that we can trust in God's guidance and presence each day. Rather than fretting about the future, the singer chooses to focus on the present, walking beside Christ with faith that he knows what is ahead.


The refrain emphasizes this belief, asserting that while there may be many things about tomorrow that we cannot understand or control, we can have confidence in the fact that God "holds tomorrow" and is always there to guide us through whatever challenges may come our way. This resolute faith is echoed in the second verse, which describes a journey towards a place of perpetual sunshine, where all tears are wiped away and every burden is made lighter. The final verse acknowledges that the road ahead may not always be easy, but reinforces the belief that God's presence and protection will sustain us through even the darkest times.


Overall, "I Know Who Holds Tomorrow" is a testament to the power of unwavering faith, and a reminder that even in our most challenging moments, we can find hope and comfort in our relationship with God.
