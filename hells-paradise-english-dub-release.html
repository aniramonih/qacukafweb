Hells Paradise English Dub Available On Crunchyroll From 15th April Read Full Details

 Posted on August 30, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  604 words  |  Kelle Repass

Hell’s Paradise is off to a fantastic start with its debut season. The anime just started broadcasting, and it’s already the third-most watched show on the Spring schedule. The anime centres around Yamada Asaemon Sagiri, an executioner, and ninja Gabimaru as they look for the Japanese Edo period’s elixir of immortality.

Two episodes of Hell’s Paradise have already been broadcast, with Crunchyroll handling the foreign airing. The show is now only available in a subbed version, and many viewers are eagerly awaiting the release of episodes in English dub. Continue reading to learn when the wait would end.

Hell’s Paradise English Dub Release Date

On Saturday, April 15 at 1:30 PT/ 4:30 ET, Crunchyroll will debut the English dub of Hell’s Paradise. Five hours following the broadcast of the third subbed episode, this first episode in the dub will be made available. After that, the dubbed version will air once per week, two weeks after the primary series.

Later, the animation will also be dubbed into Castilian Spanish, Brazilian Spanish, French, German, Italian, Latin American Spanish, and Portuguese. At the same time as their Japanese premieres on Saturdays at 8:30 a.m. PT, subbed episodes will continue to be broadcast on Crunchyroll.

Hell’s Paradise English Dub Voice Cast

Moreover, the voice cast for the dubbed version has been revealed by Crunchyroll. Starring as Gabimaru are Marisa Duran (Horimiya and Platinum End), Matt Shipman (Sk8 The Infinity and Attack on Titan), Nazeeh Tarsha (Revenger and A3), and Alejandro Saab (Horimiya and Platinum End).

In addition, Ben Balmaceda (One Piece, Aharen-san), Reagan Murdock (Chainsaw Man, Natsume’s Book of Friends), Cassie Ewulu (Demon Slayer), Jordan Dash Cruz (Trapped in a Dating Sim, Ensemble Stars), and Jill Harris (The Quintessential Quintuplets, Yuruziha, Shion, and Senta) all appear in Hell’s Paradise.

What is Hell’s Paradise About?

Based on the well-known manga, Hell’s Paradise tells the tale of Gabimaru The Hollow, a former Shinobi who has been given the death penalty for betraying his clan and breaking the law. No manner of execution can put an end to Gabimaru, who seems dispassionate and wants to pass away.

A renowned executioner named Sagiri notices Gabimaru and offers him a job that might help save him. After realising that he wants to live for his wife, Gabimaru accepts the position. As Gabimaru and Sagiri travel to a treacherous island in search of the elixir of life from strong immortals and deadly animals, the plot takes a terrible turn.

“During the Edo period in Japan, the Shogunate of Japan offers a chosen group of prisoners facing death the option to be pardoned and set free in exchange for travelling to a recently found island southwest of the Ryukyu Kingdom. According to the official narrative, the prisoners are protected by special guards to prevent escape.

As they arrive, they are confronted by the inhabitants of the island, a large number of monsters, as well as their creators, a group of immortal beings. In order to halt the immortals and leave the island, the prisoners and the guards must now cooperate.
