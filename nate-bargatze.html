Professional Life

Nate Bargatze is a rising stand-up comedian who is selling out shows across the United States. Nate joined the entertainment business following the footsteps of his dad, who is a former clown turned world-class magician. Nate’s dad’s influence is clearly seen on his 2015 debut special Full-Time Magic and his debut album Yelled at By a Clown. Additionally, Nate also won New York’s Comedy Festival and the Boston Comedy Festival in the same year.

Moreover, Nate’s half-hour Netflix Special The Standups premiered on 4 July 2017 to rave reviews. Additionally, he toured the country as a headliner and also in arenas with Chris Rock on his 2017 Total Blackout Tour. Further, Nate currently has a deal with 20th Century Fox to develop his own TV show.

In addition to all these, Nate’s comedy is both clean and relatable which is evident in his appearances on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon six times. It also followed with four appearances on Late Night with Jimmy Fallon. Nate also appeared on Conan four times and was a recurring guest on Midnight. Nate also had his own Comedy Central Presents in the year 2011. Further, Nate became one of the first comedians to perform on The Tonight Show starring Jimmy Fallon.

Talking about Nate Bargatze’s off-screen image, he was a part of Jimmy Fallon’s Clean Cut Comedy Tour. Nate also did live shows for the troops for Allied Forces in Iraq and Kuwait over five times. Nate then regularly performs at Bonnaroo, SXSW, Moontower, Oddball Comedy Festival, Sasquatch, and Clusterfest. Additionally, Nate also performed at JFL Montreal Comedy Festival where he received critical acclaim multiple years in a row.
