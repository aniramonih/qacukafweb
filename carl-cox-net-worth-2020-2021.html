Early Life & Biography

Carl Cox was born on July 29, 1962, in Oldham, England. He was inclined towards the world of music while he was still a teenager. He made his career debut around the same time as Chicago House Music finding it’s forefront in the world of dance music. Carl Cox was later on known as the founders of the sound and brought the emerging rave scene in Britain, revolutionizing the modern-day electronic music.

Career

The DJ Magazine placed Carl on the 1st position in the top 100 polls in 1997. He created his record label titled Intec Digital in 1998. It was responsible for releasing numerous hits from 1999 to 2006 and in becoming the best in dance music. Carl then put Intec Digital on a halt for some time before relaunching it in 2010.
