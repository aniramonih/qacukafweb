FAQs about King Kong Bundy

Who was King Kong Bundy?

King Kong Bundy, born Christopher Alan Pallies, was an iconic American professional wrestler known for his massive size, bald head, and demanding a five-count for pinfalls, showcasing his dominance in the ring.

What was his signature move?

Bundy’s signature move was the Avalanche Splash, which he used to finish off his opponents by leveraging his considerable weight and size.

How tall was he and how much did he weigh?

Bundy stood at 6 ft 4 in and weighed 458 lbs, earning him the nickname walking condominium from commentator Gorilla Monsoon due to his massive size.
