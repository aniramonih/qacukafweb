About Karol G

Known professionally as Karol G, Carolina Giraldo Navarro is a Colombian singer celebrated for her unique music style that blends reggaeton and Latin trap. She is also recognized for her versatility, exploring genres like reggae and sertanejo. In 2018, Karol G won the Latin Grammy Award for Best New Artist, a significant accomplishment in her career. With multiple nominations for awards such as the Billboard Latin Music Awards and Lo Nuestro Awards, Karol G has established herself as a prominent figure in Latin music.

Originally from Medellín, Colombia, Giraldo began her musical journey in her teenage years by appearing on the Colombian version of The X Factor. She later moved to New York City in 2014 and signed with Universal Music Latino, leading to a successful collaboration with Puerto Rican rapper Bad Bunny on the hit track “Ahora Me Llama.” This collaboration served as the lead single for her debut album, “Unstoppable,” released in 2017.
