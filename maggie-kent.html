Maggie Kent Bio | Wiki

Maggie Kent is an American anchor and reporter. She currently serves as a news anchor at 6abc. Maggie became part of the 6ABC Action News team in April 2018. As a reporter, she covers breaking, feature, as well as general assignment stories. Maggie began her career as a multimedia journalist as well as a reporter for News 12 The Bronx and Brooklyn. She later became the evening anchor.

While there, Maggie was nominated for an Emmy Award due to her series “Wheels in Motion.” The story was about the lives of two paralyzed New York citizens who helped each other regain their mobility. In 2016, Maggie became part of the news team at WCBS based in New York City. There, she covered numerous stories such as the 2016 U.S.A Presidential Debate that was held at Hofstra University, storm coverage, terrorist attacks, and also countless local and international issues.
