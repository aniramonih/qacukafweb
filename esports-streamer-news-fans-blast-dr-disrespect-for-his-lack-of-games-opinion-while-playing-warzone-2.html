Dr DisRespect seems to have put himself under hot waters for his take on the current scenario of the video gaming industry as he believes that there are no other games to play right now, making it quite boring while livestreaming. This ‘rant’ came recently when he was playing Call of Duty Warzone 2.

The streamer once again showed his aggressive side while playing the battle-royale game as he screamed, “There’s nothing else to play, I wanna play something, I wanna have fun.” This was highlighted by eSports journalist Jake Lucky on his recent Twitter post, to showcase how content creators have been unhappy with the current available games in the market.

The tweet made by prominent Valorant player Shroud was also brought forward as the streamer stated that he wishes to play at least one good shooter video game. However, the opinions kept by these notable streamers is being brushed aside by other gamers who feel that there are plenty of games out there.

Fans point out the mistake in Dr DisRespect’s opinion

Herschel Guy Beahm or Dr DisRespect is one of the most renowned gaming content creators in the world. This is primarily due to his unbiased opinions on different video games and being unapologetic while pointing out the flaws of any game that he streams. Even though one would find the Two-Time mostly playing Call of Duty, he has currently started to stream more games.

From The Callisto Protocol to Bioshock, he has shared his views on different games and also shared his plans to play Splatoon 3, the game which was able to defeat Modern Warfare 2. On the flip side, his fans have been curious enough to know his opinions on different games.

However, when he recently claimed that there no good games that he could play right now, fans totally disagreed with him. Several users started suggesting games that the streamers could try such as some indie games or Old School RuneScape, which according to some players can always remain as the game where players can return after playing some mainstream games.

Here are some Twitter reactions that followed after the post of Dr DisRespect

Some users were quite critical of the content creator’s opinion as they wrote that in reality, the content creators are boring rather than the games. Another user wrote, “This is what happens when you only play the games that’ll get you the most views, not the ones that interest you.”

Even though Dr DisRespect might complain about lack of games right now, everyone would be looking forward to DEADROP, the vertical shooter developed by the Two-Time himself.
