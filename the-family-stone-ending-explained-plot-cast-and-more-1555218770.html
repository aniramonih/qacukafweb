The Family Stone Plot 

In the made-up town of Thayer in New England, the film tells the story of Everett Stone and his lively family. Everett's girlfriend, Meredith, is educated but nervous, feeling uneasy about spending Christmas with his family. The tight-knit Stone family reacts awkwardly and coldly to Meredith, making her feel like an outsider. Only Ben, Everett's brother, seems to like her.
