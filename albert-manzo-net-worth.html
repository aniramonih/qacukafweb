What is Albert Manzo's Net Worth?

Albert Manzo is a New Jersey-based businessman who has a net worth of $6 million. That is a combined net worth with his wife of many years, fellow restaurateur and reality star, Caroline Manzo. Caroline is a former star of the Bravo reality television series, The Real Housewives of New Jersey. Together they co-own and manages The Brownstone, a high-end event and catering service in New Jersey. His brother Tommy Manzo is also a partner. Tommy is married to Caroline's younger sister, Dina Manzo. The Brownstone has been owned by the family for over 40 years years and is one of the premier event facilities in the state.

Albert and Caroline currently live in Franklin Lakes, New Jersey. They have three children together; Albie, Lauren and Chris Manzo. The Manzo family also had a spin-off show on Bravo called, Manzo'd With Children.
