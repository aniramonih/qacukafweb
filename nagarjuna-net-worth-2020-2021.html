Early Life

Nagarjuna was born as Akkineni Nagarjuna in the year 1959 on August 29th. He was born in Chennai in Tamil Nadu in a family where his father was an actor, as well. Akkineni is the son of Nageswara Rao Akkineni and Annapoorna Akkineni.

However, Nagarjuna and his family shifted to Hyderabad, and he is an educated fellow who earned a BS in Automobile Engineering. Akkineni Nagarjuna had acting in his blood, and he started appearing on television at an early age.

Akkineni has been married twice. His first marriage was with Lakshmi Daggubati in the year 1984. In the year 1990, they both got divorced, and Akkineni married Amala Akkineni. Amala and Nagarjuna have a son together! Akhil Akkineni is the son of Nagarjuna, and he is engaged to Shriya Bhupal, who comes from an influential family.

Career

Nagarjuna started acting at an early age. He started as a child artist in the year 1967. Since Nagarjuna’s father was a veteran actor, it was easy for the child to get entry into the movie industry. The first appearance was in a Telegu movie called Sudigundalu. Nagarjuna’s father was the lead hero in the movie.

After many years, Nagarjuna entered the movie industry with his debut film, ‘Vikram.’ Nagarjuna had a great start in the movie industry, and this was just the beginning. Some of Nagarjuna’s finest movies were, Agni Varsha, Annamacharya, LOC Kargil, Sri Ramadasu, Geetanjali, Shirdi Sai and many more. In a nutshell, he is a huge star in the movie industry.
