What Happened to MultiVersus? 

As of June 25th, 2023, MultiVersus, the WB-themed free-to-play Super Smash Bros.-like brawler, has been taken offline. The open beta for the game has been shut down ahead of its planned full release, which is targeted for early 2024.

During the open beta phase, players had the opportunity to experience the game and provide feedback to the developers at Player First Games. The developers used this period to gather valuable insights into the game's performance, mechanics, and overall player experience.

After the open beta, the decision was made to take the game offline temporarily. This offline period allows the developers to focus on making significant improvements and addressing any issues or concerns raised during the beta phase. The aim is to refine the game, enhance its content, and implement changes based on player feedback to create a more compelling and enjoyable gaming experience.

While MultiVersus is offline, players still have access to certain features. They can continue to play the game's training room and local matches, and they can use any characters and in-game cosmetics they have unlocked. However, online multiplayer gameplay is temporarily unavailable until the game is officially released in 2024.

The developers have assured players that any progress made during the open beta will carry over when the game returns in 2024. However, it's important to note that refunds won't be provided due to the closure, which might be disappointing for players who have spent money on in-game purchases.

The decision to temporarily shut down the open beta and take the game offline was likely driven by the developers' commitment to delivering a high-quality gaming experience. They plan to focus on content cadence, new characters, maps, and modes to provide players with more ways to enjoy the game. Additionally, they are working on updating the netcode and improving the matchmaking system to enhance the online multiplayer experience.
