NBA YoungBoy Is Single After Skai Jackson And Bhad Bhabie's Feud

 Posted on August 26, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  366 words  |  Kelle Repass

From the look of it, NBA YoungBoy isn’t dating Skai Jackson or Bhad Bhabie. The 20-year-old rapper recently confirmed, via a series of since-expired Instagram Stories, that he’s single.

“I don’t have no girlfriend. I keep my [baby mama] with me,” he wrote, according to screenshots of his Instagram Stories posted on The Shade Room‘s Instagram account. “I don’t know s**t about what y’all be talking.”

Although he didn’t confirm exactly what he was referring to, fans were quick to speculate that his message was a direct response to the current feud between former Disney Channel starlet Skai and rapper Bhad Bhabie — whose real name is Danielle Bregoli.

For those who missed, NBA YoungBoy found himself dragged into a social media fight between the two teenagers on February 24, 2020, when Bhad Bhabie seemingly threatened the Jessie actress during an Instagram Live and on her Instagram Stories. Bhad Bhabie accused Skai of “talking s**t” about her on her private Instagram account, blocking her on social media and flirting with her ex-boyfriend, NBA YoungBoy.

“Why are you so obsessed with me, b***h? I will literally kill you,” Bhad Bhabie said during the livestream. “I don’t know why you’re not understanding that. You’re so small, if I get my hands on you… it’s over, you’re going to the hospital. Get all the money you can, get all the shows and commercials you can because when I get ahold of you, you ain’t gonna be doing none of that s**t no more.”

Days after their public feud, on February 27, 2020, the Superior Court of California in Los Angeles granted Skai a temporary restraining order against Bhad Bhabie. According to People, the restraining order requires Bhad Bhabie stay at least 100 yards away from the former Disney Channel star. Additionally, she is not allowed to have any contact with Skai, even on social media. The court also set a hearing date for March 20, 2020.

NBA YoungBoy has yet to confirm his relationship with either Skai or Bhad Bhabie, but he did let fans know that right now, he’s 100 percent single.
