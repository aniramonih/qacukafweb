What Is This Astroneer?

Astroneer is a sandbox adventure game developed by System Era Softworks. Players take on the role of an astronaut on a distant planet, where they have to explore and survive while collecting resources, building structures, and researching new technologies.

The game features a procedurally generated open world, with a focus on research, discovery, and crafting. Players can work together to build bases and research new technologies, or work out on their own to discover new areas and uncover hidden secrets.
