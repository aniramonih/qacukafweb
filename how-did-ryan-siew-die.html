How did Ryan Siew Die?

Ryan the popular guitarist died unfortunately at an very short age. But his perfect death reasons are not open yet. The group is famous to forget everything when they sing. They are just lost in their music. 

Members Jamie Hails, Rick Schneider, Jake Steinhauser, and Daniel Furnari wrote on Instagram: 

“It is with shattered hearts & the deepest sorrow that we tell you our dear brother and bandmate Ryan Siew passed away on the morning of Monday, June 19. He was 26 years old, and for 10 incredible years, he was our best friend and artistic soulmate. Those years will never be enough.”

“He was kindhearted and clever, he was funny and brave and creative, and he was talented beyond all measure. He loved art, and beauty in all its forms.”

Five months ago he shared a picture on Instagram while he was in hospital captioned with the new year “working on myself.” “I’m happy to say I’m no longer on any antidepressants, no longer living with the constant brain fog and lethargy,” he shared. Because of Polaris’s death, they canceled all their concert and European summer tour and said, “a serious personal crisis in our family.”

The band also stated that “Most of all, he loved & adored his family & friends. He was also much admired and beloved by so many. Ryan, we will love and miss you for the rest of our days, and we will never fill the hole that you leave in all of our lives.”
