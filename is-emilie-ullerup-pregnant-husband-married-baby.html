Is Emilie Ullerup Pregnant Again?

No, the Danish Actress Emilie Ullerup is not pregnant and has not revealed any news on her pregnancy. However, the actress was pregnant and recently completed her maternity stage.

She recalls the time when she was pregnant during the shoot of ‘Nature of Love.’ According to the actress, she was four months pregnant and had a difficult time hiding her swollen belly on the screen and gives all the credit to the film crew members who had helped her in the shoot.

Similarly, the actress managed not to do any of the action scenes for the movie at the time of her pregnancy. She skipped the scenes, and the production had replaced her during action shots and completed the movie.

Emilie describes the experience of being pregnant and doing a movie as a difficult task but fun at the same time. She mentions that the feeling of being pregnant gave her inner happiness, while doing her work during pregnancy is sometimes stressful.

Emilie Ullerup Husband Kyle Cassie- Married

The Nature of Love actress Emilie Ullerup is married to Kyle Cassie. The couple started dating back in the year 2007 from the sets of a Canadian TV series jPod and decided to get married in 2018.

Her husband Kyle is a British Columbian director and filmmaker mostly known for ‘Lost Boys 2’. He was really happy to see his lovely wife continuing her passion for movies even at the time of her pregnancy.

Kyle and Ullerup both have been through a life-threatening moment. Kyle had a major car accident at the age of 19, which took him to a coma, but he was lucky that he survived, and his wife Emilie suffered from a tumor around her spine which took her about a year to recover.

Emilie Ullerup & Kyle Cassie blessed with a Baby Boy

The actress Emilie Ullerup recently gave birth to her first child, a baby boy, earlier this year in January 2021. The baby was born healthy and is named Wilde Morkov Ullerup Cassie.

The couple had shared the news through their social media handle. Emilie often shares about her newly born baby on her Instagram profile which is under the username emilie.ullerup
