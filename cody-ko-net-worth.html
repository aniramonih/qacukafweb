FAQs about Cody Ko

Who is Cody Ko?

Cody Ko is a Canadian YouTuber, podcaster, comedian, and rapper known for his crudely comedic and profane content style. As of April 2023, his five YouTube channels have collectively earned over 9.37 million subscribers and 1.86 billion views.

How did he become famous?

His rise to internet fame began in 2013 when he started uploading short skits on Vine, becoming popular among fans and followers. His career further skyrocketed with the launch of his YouTube channel in 2014.

What did Cody Ko study?

He majored in computer science at Duke University. After completing his education, he initially worked as a software developer before venturing full-time into social media and content creation.

Is Cody Ko married?

Yes, he is married to Kelsey Kreppel. The couple tied the knot on February 4, 2023, at the Sands Hotel and Spa in Indian Wells, California, in a ceremony officiated by their close friend Adam Cue.

Where is Cody Ko from?

What other ventures is Cody Ko involved in?

Besides his YouTube career, he is also part of the comedy-rap duo Tiny Meat Gang with Noel Miller. They have released music and host a podcast together. Cody has also ventured into acting and entrepreneurship.

What is his educational background?
