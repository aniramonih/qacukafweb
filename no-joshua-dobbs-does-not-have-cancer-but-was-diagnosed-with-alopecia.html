No, Joshua Dobbs Does Not Have Cancer But Was Diagnosed With Alopecia At The Age Of 10

Many of his fans believed that he had Cancer after he was blad; Dobbs started losing hair when he was ten.

Dobbs then learned that he was diagnosed with Alopecia when he started his college career. 

Alopecia aerate is a condition of Hair loss that can affect just your scalp or your entire body, and it can be temporary or permanent.

Heredity, hormonal changes, medical conditions, or a normal part of aging are some causes of Alopecia aerate.

For Dobbs, it did not cause only the hair on his head, but it also caused hair o his face and other parts of his body.

The player has always been firm about his condition and used his place to make people aware of his health condition, which is typical. 

Dobbs’ message to young people who may be affected by the condition is equally inspirational.

He has always taken the health condition very lightly, and it was part of his life; he mentioned that it was hard to cope with it at first, but now this is a moving part of his life.

Dobbs once said, “Keep in mind that all your dreams can be accomplished without your hair. Alopecia shouldn’t stand in your way. You live in a society filled with social media and a focus on beauty. But at the end of the day, if you are comfortable in your skin and have long or no hair, you can be successful and happy.”
