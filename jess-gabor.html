Jess Gabor (born October 23, 1996) is an American Actress, Writer, and Social Media Personality from Los Angeles, California. She rose to fame after starring as Kelly Keefe in the television series “Shameless”.

It is a comedy-drama TV show which is an adaptation of Paul Abbott’s British series of the same name developed by John Wells. Furthermore, Gabor featured in the 9th season of the show in 2018.

Later, the actress reprised her part in the Shameless Season 10 whose first episode aired on November 10, 2019. Some of the other main casts of the show are William H. Macy, Jeremy Allen White, Noel Fisher, Cameron Monaghan, etc.
