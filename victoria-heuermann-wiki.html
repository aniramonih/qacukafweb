Gilgo Beach Case

When the first set of human remains was discovered near Oak Beach’s Ocean Parkway, the serene shores of Gilgo Beach were transformed into a haunting crime scene. As more bodies were discovered, the community was rocked by shockwaves. The disappearance of Shannan Gilbert, whose distressed 911 call indicated imminent danger, threw the investigation into disarray. Officer John Mallia made a breakthrough by unearthing Melissa Barthelemy’s remains with the help of his cadaver dog Blue. Three more bodies were discovered nearby after additional searches.

Several law enforcement agencies quickly formed a task force to investigate the Gilgo Beach murders, pooling their expertise and resources. When his DNA was discovered on a partially consumed pizza near the crime scene, the investigation focused on Rex Heuermann. Cellphone records, analysis of burner phones, and incriminating Google searches all implicated him in the crimes. On July 13, 2023, Heuermann was arrested after a year-long investigation.
