Kim Porter's Sons Quincy and King Remember Their Late Mom with Sweet Tributes

 Posted on August 14, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  449 words  |  Kelle Repass

Diddy's children Quincy and King remember their late mom Kim Porter during Mother's Day, sharing heartwarming tributes for her on Instagram.

It's never easy to lose a loved one, most especially a mother. For Kim Porter and Diddy's children, they've had to make do without their mom, and they make sure to remember her in every way they can.

On Mother's Day, her two sons Quincy and King led the way with their Mother's Day greetings, letting everyone know just how much they miss their mom. Nonetheless, they also knew that their mom was watching over their every move.

KING'S MOTHER'S DAY MESSAGE

King's greeting featured him giving his mom a bouquet of roses as he blew her a kiss. Aside from letting her know just how much he loves and misses her, he also shared how not a day goes by without him thinking of her.

"All I wanna do is just look at you and give you hugs and kisses and eat your friend chicken. I love you miss you and know you watching over me."

QUINCY'S MOTHER'S DAY MESSAGE

Meanwhile, Quincy shared a selfie of him and his mom, as they both blew kisses to the camera. Unlike King's long message, he kept his short and sweet all while sharing the same message.

"I celebrate you every second of every day. I miss you & love you mommy."

MOURNING THEIR LOSS

It's not a new thing for Porter's proud sons to share tributes to her on social media, as they've been posting them ever since her death back on November 15, 2018. It was a difficult experience for them, and writing to their late mom on social media was a way for them to express their feelings.

Recently, Diddy unveiled a life-sized replica of Kim Porter, which he commissioned three years before her death.

Diddy delivering his acceptance speech at the Clive Davis Pre-Grammy Gala in January 2020. | Photo: Getty Images/GlobalImagesUkraine

Quincy Brown is Porter's son from her relationship with Al B. Sure!, but he was raised by Sean "Diddy" Combs. Meanwhile, Combs and Porter share three children, Christian, and their twin daughters D'Lila Star and Jessie James.

REMEMBERING KIM PORTER

Recently, Diddy unveiled a life-sized replica of Kim Porter, which he commissioned three years before her death. It now stands in his backyard, and is a constant reminder of the woman he loves.

Although Diddy and Porter were not together during the time of her death, the rapper claims that she was and will always be his soulmate. For as long as he lives, he promised to take care of their children on their behalf and that's something he continues to do till today.
