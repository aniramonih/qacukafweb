C.J. Watson at the Center of Floyd Mayweather Arrest, Alleged Assault

 Posted on August 13, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  284 words  |  Tandra Barner

When shooting guard C.J. Watson signed with the Chicago Bulls in the off-season, the team hoped he’d provide some scoring punch off the bench. We doubt this is what they had in mind.

According to a newly-released police report, the career 82% foul shooter was sleeping with Josie Harris, the ex-girlfriend boxer Floyd Mayweather stands accused of hitting.

The documents state that Mayweather read text messages on his baby mama’s phone, a couple of which were from Watson and screamed: “Are you having sex with C.J.?” Harris’ affirmative answer led to the assault in question.

You might wanna work on your defense, C.J. Watson.

The report, obtained by TMZ, gets even more disturbing: it reads that the 10-year-old son of Mayweather and Harris told police he “saw his dad was on his mother and was hitting and kicking her.”

From there, “Harris stated that Mayweather began yelling at her, ‘I’m going to kill you and the man your (sic) are messing around with… I’m going to have you both disappear.'”

This would damage Chicago’s Eastern Conference title hopes. You gotta have quality back-up play at the two spot.

 These legal documents confirm that Harris – who called 911 around 5 a.m. Thursday morning – was taken to Southern Hills Hospital and treated for “minor facial injuries and a sore left arm.” 

Mayweather has not been charged yet with any form of assault. Instead, he was booked for for grand larceny on Friday, accused of taking Josie’s Apple iPhone 4, valued at $322.17.

** UPDATE: Says Watson’s father: “I know for sure that Josie is not his girlfriend… she did some event planning for C.J., but other than that, they have no relationship whatsoever.”
