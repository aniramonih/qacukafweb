Who is Elizabeth Vargas?

Elizabeth Vargas is a renowned American television journalist known for her work on ABC’s 20/20 and World News Tonight. She has won multiple awards, including Emmys and a Peabody, for her outstanding journalism. Vargas currently hosts Elizabeth Vargas Reports on NewsNation and a true crime series called iCrime with Elizabeth Vargas.

How did Elizabeth Vargas start her career?

Vargas began her career in journalism after graduating from the University of Missouri with a Bachelor of Journalism degree. She started as a reporter and anchor for local news stations before joining NBC News in 1993. In 1996, she moved to ABC News, where she became a prominent figure.
