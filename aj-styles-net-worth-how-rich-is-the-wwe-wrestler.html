AJ Styles Early Life

AJ Styles grew up with three other siblings in Base Camp, Lejeune, Jacksonville, North Carolina. Details about his siblings and parents are kept private for undisclosed reasons.

His family was later relocated to Gainesville, North Carolina. AJ Styles spent the majority of his childhood in a trailer park, where he was impoverished.

Furthermore, Styles completed his high school years at Johnson High School using whatever means he could.

Nonetheless, he was a bright young man who began playing basketball, football, and baseball in eighth grade. Styles used to drive an ambulance and mow lawns to supplement his family’s income after college, and he soon joined a wrestling school with a friend.

AJ Styles Net Worth & Career

Styles made his WWE debut in 2016, winning the WWE Championship. Styles, dubbed “The Phenomenal One,” continued to make a name for himself on the international stage, winning the WWE Intercontinental Championship and the WWE United States Championship three times.

However, unlike other professional sports such as basketball and soccer, the salaries of WWE wrestlers are not publicly disclosed. When doing business with the company, each individual acts as an independent contractor.

Styles began his career in various wrestling promotions, including the NWA, WCW, WWF, and TNA, before rising to stardom with WWE. One of his defining moments came when he joined Ring of Honor (ROH) and quickly rose to the top of the roster, fighting in several championship matches.

Styles also had the opportunity to compete against a young CM Punk, defeating him twice. According to Essentially Sports, his time with TNA enabled him to win the first-ever X-Division Championship.
