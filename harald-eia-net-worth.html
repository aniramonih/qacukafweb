What is he known for?

Eia is best known for his work on various Norwegian Broadcasting Corporation comedy TV series like Lille Lørdag, Åpen Post, and Uti vår hage, among others. He has also gained recognition for his stand-up comedy, theatresports, and roles in Norwegian films.

What educational background does Harald Eia have?

He holds a Candidate’s degree in sociology from the University of Oslo, which he obtained in 1992, focusing on the symbolic struggles of business people.

What is Hjernevask and why is it significant?

Hjernevask (Brainwash) is a television show introduced by Eia in 2010, which delves into the nature vs. nurture debate by contrasting cultural determinist models with nature-nurture interactionist perspectives. The show sparked significant public debate, particularly around the topics of gender and the gender paradox in Norway.

Has his received any awards for his work?

Yes, for Hjernevask, he was awarded the Fritt Ord Honorary Award in 2010 for provoking one of the most heated debates on research in recent times.

What other projects has Harald Eia worked on?

Since 2017, Eia, along with Nils Brenna, has produced the podcast and radio series Sånn er du, where famous Norwegians take a Big 5 personality test.

Has his authored any books?

Yes, in 2020, Eia published a book discussing why Norway has become so prosperous in recent times.

What controversies has Hjernevask led to?

The show led to extensive public debate, particularly criticized by Jørgen Lorentzen for its portrayal of gender research. The debate highlighted discussions on scientific rigor and ideological biases within academia.
