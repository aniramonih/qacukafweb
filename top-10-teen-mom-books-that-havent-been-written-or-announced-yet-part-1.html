Top 10 Teen Mom books that havent been written (or announced) yet

 Posted on August 7, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  603 words  |  Tandra Barner

The cast members from all of MTV’s Teen Mom franchises are quietly building up quite a library of published books, with new ones on the way from Jenelle Evans, Maci Bookout, Matt Baier, and Javi Marroquin. But, despite all of the reading material previously published and coming soon, there are still a number of titles we’re anxiously waiting for. So, as a helpful guide, we have compiled our Top Ten Teen Mom books that haven’t been written (or announced) yet.

NOTE: When I started this post, the assumption was that I would just create a few actual book covers and have text for the others in the list. But, once I started, I realized I couldn’t not make covers for ALL of them! So, for that reason, this has taken me a little longer than expected and I will be breaking it up into two parts. But hey, at least it’s not one of those clickbait galleries, right?

OK, let the countdown begin with numbers 10-6…

9. High! High! We’re Both High! My Turbulent Life with Teen Mom 2 Star Jenelle Evans by Tori Rhyne

7. Better Call Dustin: A Guide To Getting Away With EVERYTHING by Dustin Sullivan
