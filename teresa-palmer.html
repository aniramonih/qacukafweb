Teresa Palmer is an Australian actress who has starred in movies and TV shows such as I Am Number Four, Hacksaw Ridge, Ride Like a Girl, Warm Bodies, Bedtime Stories, among many others. Palmer has also worked as a writer and producer. 

The beautiful actress was born as Teresa Mary Palmer on February 26, 1986, in Adelaide, South Australia, to an investor Kevin Palmer (father), and the former nurse Paula Sanders (mother). 

Regarding education, Teresa graduated from an independent Catholic school named Mercedes College.

Celebrity Husband: Mark Webber

Like Teresa, her husband Mark Webber is also a member of the Hollywood Film Industry. The 40-years-old Mark is an actor, screenwriter, and director.

Webber has portrayed his character in movies and TV series such as Medium, Hollywood Ending, Snow Day, The Even After, Save the Date, People I Know, Weapons, Stumptown, Scott Pilgrim vs the Word, etc. 

Similarly, Mark has directed the drama films such as Explicit Ills, The End of Love, Flesh and Blood, and The Place of No Words. Likewise, Mark and Teresa have also worked together as director, screenwriter, producer, and cast members in the American film The Ever After (2014).
