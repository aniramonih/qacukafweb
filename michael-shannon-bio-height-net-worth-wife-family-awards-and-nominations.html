Michael Shannon is an American actor and musician, best known for his roles in films like Man of Steel and Breakthrough Road. He received an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor for his role in Breakthrough Road in 2008 and Animal Nocturne in 2016.

Organic

Award-winning actor Michael Corbett Shannon was born in Lexington, Kentucky, USA on August 7, 1974. He was raised by his parents, Geraldine Hine, a lawyer, and Donald Shannon, an accounting professor at DePaul University.

Michael never had a good upbringing thanks to his parent’s divorce, it affected him emotionally and he reportedly scattered the psychiatrist’s office he was sent to for therapy.

The talented star did not attend drama school, yet he was able to start his career in Chicago, where he lived with his father. As a child, he dreamed of being a jazz musician or an architect.

Career

When he was sixteen, Michael found a mentor named Tracy Letts who coached him on stage techniques. Later, he started playing small roles in films. He made his first appearance on Groundhog Day in 1993, and in 1996 he featured in the movie called Punaise.

Michael Shannon had his breakthrough in 2008 after starring in Breakthrough Road. The film, however, earned him an Oscar nomination for Best Supporting Actor. He also won the Screen Actors Guild Award for Best Ensemble for his role in the reality television series titled Boardwalk Empire which aired from 2010 to 2014. The talented actor was also recognized for his role in the thriller titled Sheltering in 2011.

The American actor has acted in several films and received several accolades for his brilliant and excellent roles in the films. Some of his popular movies include Ice Man in 2012, Man of Steel in 2013, 99 Houses in 2014, Wolves in 2016, Guillermo Del Toro Sales Shape of Water in 2017, The Price in 2018 and many more.

Musical career

After his passion for music, Michael Shannon formed a rock band called Corporel in 2002. Since the band’s inception, Michael has been composing and singing with the band. Rob Beitzel and Ray Rizzo are also part of the group. The group of corporals released their first album in 2010, one of the songs of which was dedicated to the re-election of President Obama.

Awards and nominations

Michael Corbett has been nominated for numerous awards, including Academy Awards—twice for Best Supporting Actor—for his role in Breakthrough Road. He also received nominations for Actors Guild Award and Golden Globe Award, as well as Tony Award for Featured Actor in a Play for his brilliant role in the film titled Long Day Into Night

He also won eighteen out of eighteen nominations and he worked with famous directors including Guillermo Del Toro, a top director in the Hollywood film industry.

Personal life: wife and family

Michael had a relationship with another celebrity, an American actress called Kate Arrington. Kate is famous for playing Courtney in the movie titled Hello I Gotta Go and the popular reality series titled Elementary

The couple have been together since 2002 and have never been married, although they have two children, Sylvia Shannon and Marion Shannon.

Michael and his family live together in Red Hook, New York.
