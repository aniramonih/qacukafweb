One of his notable milestones is the release of his twelfth studio album, "There's Really a Wolf," which debuted in May 2017. This album marked a pivotal moment in his career, showcasing his talents as an artist.

Russ was born on September 26, 1992, in Secaucus, New Jersey, United States. His upbringing in New Jersey influenced his musical style and lyrical content.

Russ initially gained attention by independently releasing his music for free on various online platforms. His consistent and high-quality releases garnered a significant following and helped him secure a record deal.
