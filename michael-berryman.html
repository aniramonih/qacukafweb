Age, Biography and Wiki

Michael Berryman is an American actor and producer best known for his roles in horror films such as The Hills Have Eyes (1977), Weird Science (1985), and The Devil's Rejects (2005). He has also appeared in television shows such as The X-Files, Star Trek: Deep Space Nine, and The Twilight Zone. Berryman was born in Los Angeles, California, on September 4, 1948. He was born with hypohidrotic ectodermal dysplasia, a rare genetic disorder that affects the development of teeth, hair, and sweat glands. As a result, he has a distinctive appearance, with a bald head and no eyebrows or eyelashes. Berryman began his acting career in the 1970s, appearing in films such as The Hills Have Eyes (1977) and Escape from New York (1981). He has since appeared in numerous films and television shows, including Weird Science (1985), The X-Files (1993-2002), Star Trek: Deep Space Nine (1993-1999), and The Devil's Rejects (2005). Berryman has also worked as a producer, most notably on the horror film The Hills Have Eyes Part II (1984). He has also served as a transportation coordinator on films such as The Devil's Rejects (2005) and The Hills Have Eyes (2006).
