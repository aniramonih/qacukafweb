WWE live events in Texas headlined by cage matches (correspondents needed), TNA Impact Wrestling TV

 Posted on August 20, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  472 words  |  Tandra Barner
By Jason Powell

-Dot Net Members will be listening to the second part of the Dot Net Weekly audio show with Chris Shore and I, and Will Pruett's TNA Impact Wrestling audio review later today. The Members are already listening to the 58-Minute Dot Net Countdown with Will Pruett and Jake Barnett. If you enjoy our All Access audio, help support Dot Net by signing up for membership to the ad-free version of the website via the Dot Net Members' Signup Page.

-TNA is taping future editions of the Impact Wrestling television show tonight and Saturday at Universal Studios in Orlando, Florida. We are looking for correspondents for the tapings. If you are going and want to help, email me at dotnetjason@gmail.com.

-We are looking for correspondents for the WWE live events listed below. If you are attending one of the shows listed below and want to help, send your report (or basic results) to dotnetjason@gmail.com.

-WWE is in Waco, Texas at Extraco Events Center tonight with the following lineup: Randy Orton vs. John Cena in a cage match for the WWE World Heavyweight Championship, The Shield vs. The Wyatt Family, Sheamus vs. Alberto Del Rio, The Uso Brothers vs. Cody Rhodes and Goldust vs. The Real Americans in a Triple Threat tag match (presumably for the WWE Tag Team Championship).

-WWE is in Laredo, Texas at Laredo Energy Center on Saturday with the following talent: Daniel Bryan, Kane, Christian, The Shield, The Wyatt Family, Big E, Big Show, Fandango, The Miz, and Dolph Ziggler.

-WWE is in Austin, Texas at Frank Erwin Center on Saturday with the following lineup: Randy Orton vs. John Cena in a cage match for the WWE Championship, an Sheamus vs. Alberto Del Rio.

-WWE is in Corpus Christi, Texas at American Bank Center on Sunday with the following lineup: Daniel Bryan vs. Kane in a cage match, Big E vs. The Miz for the Intercontinental Championship, A.J. Lee vs. Cameron for the Divas Championship, The Uso Brothers vs. Cody Rhodes and Goldust vs. The Real Americans in a Triple Threat tag match.

-WWE is in Dallas, Texas at American Airlines Center on Sunday with the following lineup for a matinee show: Randy Orton vs. John Cena in a cage match for the WWE World Heavyweight Championship, The Shield vs. The Wyatt Family, Sheamus vs. Alberto Del Rio.

-WWE wrestler Santino Marella is 35 today. He was born on March 14, 1979.

-Former WWE and WCW wrestler Bryan Clark is 50 today. He was born on March 14, 1964.

-Former ECW valet Beulah McGillicutty (a/k/a Trisa Laughlin) is 45 today. She was born on March 14, 1969. She is married to Tommy Dreamer an the couple has twin daughters. In fact, the twins appeared in The Sopranos season six episode "The Ride" as Tony Soprano's baby niece.
