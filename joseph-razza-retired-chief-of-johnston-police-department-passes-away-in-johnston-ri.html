Joseph Razza, Retired Chief of Johnston Police Department passes away in Johnston, RI

 Posted on August 17, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  559 words  |  Kelle Repass

The Johnston, Rhode Island community is in mourning following the sudden passing of Police Chief Joseph Razza, who tragically departed this life on Friday, September 29, 2023. Chief Razza’s unexpected demise has left countless hearts devastated, as the news of his death reverberated through the town, leaving a deep sense of loss in its wake.

Chief Razza, not just a dedicated public servant but also a cherished friend and guide to many, was confirmed dead on the aforementioned date, as announced by the Johnston Police Department in a heartfelt social media post. The outpouring of condolences from the community has been overwhelming, a testament to the impact Chief Razza had on the lives of those he touched.

His legacy is monumental, his contributions to the safety and well-being of the community immeasurable. Chief Razza’s leadership left an indelible mark on the Johnston Police Department, inspiring all who worked alongside him. In this time of sorrow, the public stands in solidarity with Chief Razza’s wife, Mandi, along with his family and friends, sharing in their grief and offering unwavering support during this trying period.

Lance Przybyla  shared a post on fb on the death of Joseph:

This world lost a tremendous Man today…far to soon. Since I met him he was determined to be a State Trooper for Rhode island…. unfortunately at the time politics played to much of factor then….. upon graduation of college..he would not be deterred…he continued his goal with the police academy…graduating and then to the Wyatt detention center…as he grew and gained experience he was determined to further his career and landed a spot with the Jamestown Police department… working any and all shifts… providing support and unbeknownst to him displaying his leadership ability with class and dignity and with that fortunately afforded him an opportunity to become an officer at his home town of Johnston Rhode Island…where he grew up…graduated high school and his parents still resided. Due to his continued determination to be the best in his field to lead and serve and was awarded the opportunity to become Chief of Police of the Johnston Police department…where he led and served for far to short of a time…I know from talking to residents and a few officers he was extremely respected and welcomed into this position. Joe was a tremendous individual… calm…fair… determined… loyal… family first….goal oriented person….I was fortunate to meet him during the high school years and continued on into our professional careers….the amount of fun we had from working out..going to the north end…that pizza beer joint in the woods…fishing..renting boats..Corvettes and many other events we did together were magnanimous…..God bless you Joe
Joseph Razza rest in peace my friend….

As of now, the circumstances surrounding Chief Joseph Razza’s passing remain undisclosed, leaving the community in suspense. The exact details of his demise are unknown, with no further information available at this time. The public anxiously awaits updates, and further details will be provided as soon as they are made available.

During this somber period, the community is urged to keep the entire Razza family in their thoughts and prayers, offering solace and strength as they navigate through this difficult time of grief. Chief Razza’s legacy will live on, not just in the Johnston Police Department but in the hearts of all those he touched throughout his impactful life.
