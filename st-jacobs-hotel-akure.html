St Jacobs Hotel, Akure

 Posted on August 26, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  132 words  |  Patria Henriques

St Jacobs Hotel is located at Block 1, Plot 7A and B, off Leaonac Junction, Alagbaka GRA, Akure, Ondo State.

We have 44 rooms in total. (25 Executive Deluxe, 4 Royal Deluxe, 10 Royal Superior, 4 Diplomatic Suite and 1 Presidential Suite) complimentary breakfast inclusive.

We have a swimming pool and a well-equipped gym for fitness.

Electricity is sustained by 3 powerful generating sets.

Laundry services are available for both in house and external guest.

24-hour security is provided by a team of well trained personnel.

We also would like to point out we have a mini hall of a 50 persons capacity in a classroom setting.

St Jacobs Hotel is a luxury hotel in Akure,
Ondo.
