Who is Traylor Howard?

The gorgeous actress first came into this world on the 14th day of June 1966 and was given Traylor Elizabeth Howard at birth. A native of Orlando in Florida, United States, Traylor is the daughter of Robert and Peggy Howard. While nothing much is known about her mother, it is known that her father is the President of the Howard Fertilizer and Chemical Company. The actress possesses American nationality and is of white ethnicity.

While she was growing, Traylor Howard was enrolled into Lake Highland Preparatory School situated in Orlando, Florida. There, she completed her high school education and after graduation, she proceeded to Florida State University where she acquired a degree in Advertising and Communications with a minor in English.

Her Career Achievements

Traylor went on to begin her acting career soon after she graduated from college. She made her first film appearance with a role in the 1994 movie, Till the End of the Night where she played as Fran. Later in the same year, she also appeared in an episode of Lois & Clark: The New Adventures of Superman alongside Dean Cain and Teri Hatcher. From 1996 to 1997, the actress portrayed the role of Joy Byrnes in the NBC sitcom series, Boston Common along with Anthony Clark and Tasha Smith.

In the year that followed, Traylor Howard was seen playing the role of Anne Henning in the film, Confessions of a Sexist Pig. She garnered her breakthrough when she joined the cast of the ABC sitcom, Two Guys and a Girl. There, she played the recurring role of Sharon Carter from 1998 to 2001. She followed it up with minor roles in The Division, First Monday, The West Wing, and Bram and Alice in 2002.

Her next major role came in 2005 when she played as Natalie Teeger in the American comedy-drama, Monk. In the series, she starred alongside Ted Levine and Bitty Schram from 2005 to 2009. Her outstanding performance in the series fetched a Gracie Allen Award for Best Supporting Actress in a Comedy. She was last seen in 2010 when she portrayed Julie Nolan in the TV pilot, Nolan Knows Best. Since then, Traylor disappeared from the TV screens and has not been seen in any TV show or movie to date.

Traylor Howard’s Spouse and Family

The iconic actress has had her fair share of romantic relationships and has been married three times in her lifetime. Her first marital stint was with an American actor named Cameron Hall. The duo tied the knot on 2nd February 1991. However, two years later, they went their separate ways in March 1993. They didn’t have any kids in their union.

Ten years later, Traylor Howard got married a second time and this time to another actor named Christian Navarro. Their marriage happened in April 2003 and these two were married for three years before getting a divorce in 2006. Just like her first marriage, Traylor and Christian didn’t have any kids in their marriage.

Soon after their divorce was finalized, the actress gave birth to a son named Sabu in November 2006. Five years later, in 2011 she got married to a man named Jarel Portman. The duo tied the knot sometime in 2011 and the following year, they welcomed a son named Julien in 2012.

Interestingly, Traylor’s marriage with Jarel has been her longest marriage ever and the couple has been going stronger ever since. They currently reside in the United States with their two kids and there is no sign of marital issues between them.
