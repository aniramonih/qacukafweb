Andy Wilman Biography

Andy Wilman is best known as Television Producer, TV Presenter who has an estimated Net Worth of $60 Million. Singer who released seventeen Gold- and Platinum-certified albums and sang “Moon River.”  He was born on December 3,1927, Wall Lake, IA. He hosted his own show from 1962 to 1971. He performed on multiple radio stations with his brothers. He started playing music with his brothers in The Williams Brothers. He was so popular that at one time he had earned more Gold-certified albums than any solo performer except Frank Sinatra. He married Claudine Longet on December 15, 1961 and, after their divorce on February 7, 1975, he married Debbie Haas on May 3, 1991. His zodiac sign is Sagittarius
