FAQs about Noel Miller

Who is Noel Miller?

He is a Canadian-American YouTuber, comedian, rapper, podcaster, and filmmaker known for his comedy skits on Vine, co-hosting the TMG Podcast with Cody Ko, and creating the short film Suki.

How old is Noel Miller?

He is 34 years old as of 2024.

How did he start his career?

Miller kicked off his comedic career using Vine to gain attention and followers. After Vine closed, he moved to YouTube in 2015, uploading his first video titled Colgate Evergreen Ad.

What is the Tiny Meat Gang (TMG)?

In 2017, he and Cody Ko formed the comedy and rap group Tiny Meat Gang, gaining viral success with their Love Island gameplay videos and THAT’S CRINGE series. They also started the Tiny Meat Gang Podcast the same year.

What are some achievements of TMG?

The Tiny Meat Gang Podcast won the Best Podcast award at the 11th Shorty Awards in 2019.

Has he won any awards?

Yes, besides TMG Podcast’s award, Miller has been recognized for his creative work, including his contributions to the comedic and digital content creation spheres.

Is Noel Miller married?

Yes, he is married to Aleena Akhtar. The couple began dating in 2014, got engaged in October 2020, and married in October 2022.

Notable works include his YouTube comedy skits, the TMG Podcast, the Suki short film, and his musical projects, including the solo EP Push and TMG’s EPs Bangers & Ass and Locals Only.
