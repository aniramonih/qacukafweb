He is a stand-up comedian known for his nervous charm, endearing smile, and humor about life as an autistic individual and Army veteran. He comes from Levy County, Florida, and has become a significant figure in the comedy scene.

What makes him unique in the comedy world?

His comedy showcases his vibrant personality and how his mind works, especially regarding his experiences with autism. This perspective gives his humor a unique and relatable quality.

Has he won any comedy competitions?
