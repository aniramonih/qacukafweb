Bad Bunny Dominates WWE Merchandise Sales Charts

 Posted on August 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  303 words  |  Kelle Repass

Bad Bunny’s name is massive outside WWE, as one of the most popular rappers on the planet. His popularity shows in great numbers even when you’re looking at WWE’s sales charts.

Wrestlenomics reported a very interesting note behind their Patreon. It was reported that Bad Bunny pretty much blew everyone out of the water in terms of merchandise sales.

This is actually Bad Bunny’s second week on top of WWE’s sales charts, with LWO shirts remaining the top-selling item on WWEShop, closely pursued by Rhea Ripley’s “She’s My Mami” shirt.

Merchandise report: May 13 to May 19, 2023

WWEShop – Categories
1. Bad Bunny (Items in top 10 daily: 15) [reverse rank value: 62]
2. LWO (14) [89]
3. Rhea Ripley (7) [50]
4. Seth Freakin Rollins (7) [32]
5. Cody Rhodes (7) [19]
6. Matt Riddle (6) [57]
7. The Usos (5) [12]
8. Roman Reigns (3) [22]
9. Braun Strowman (2) [20]
10. Raquel Rodriguez (2) [18]

It was also noted that, “Over on AEW and PWTees, FTR was the leading brand, with their ‘Living Legends’ shirt taking the top spot. Notably, FTR also headed the AEW list in the previous week.”

We will have to see when Bad Bunny makes a return to the ring, but there is certainly a reason why WWE keeps bringing him back. His popularity brings massive numbers to WWE’s viewership and their shop.
