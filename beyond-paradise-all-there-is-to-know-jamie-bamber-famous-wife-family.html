All there is to know about Beyond Paradise star Jamie Bamber's famous wife

    Beyond Paradise fans have been loving the Death in Paradise spin-off series. One storyline which has gripped viewers is the tension between Archie Hughes, played by Jamie Bamber, and Martha Lloyd (Sally Bretton).

    Fans were stunned when Archie leaned in to kiss his former flame, only to be met with a swift slap from Martha, who is happily in a relationship with lead character Humphrey Goodman (Kris Marshall).

    Fortunately, in real life, Jamie's love life seems much more smooth sailing and he's been with his wife for over 19 years. The actor's wife is also a famous actress, here's everything you need to know about their relationship and family.

    Who is Beyond Paradise star Jamie Bamber married to?

    It's not known how Jamie and Kerry met, but they both have previous credits in the TV mini-series . Jamie was known for his role as Lee 'Apollo' Adama in the show while Kerry played the part of Layne Ishay. Elsewhere in the TV and film world, Kerry has credits in and more.
