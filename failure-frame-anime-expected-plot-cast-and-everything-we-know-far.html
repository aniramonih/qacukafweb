Failure Frame Anime: Failure Frame: I Became the Strongest and Annihilated Everything with Low-Level Spells is a Japanese light novel series penned by Kaoru Shinozaki. The story first emerged on the Shōsetsuka ni Narō website in November 2017 and later found its way into print after being acquired by Overlap. The printed version, featuring illustrations by KWKM, was released in July 2018. Following its success, a manga adaptation with a storyline by Keyaki Uchi-Uchi and illustrations by Shō Uyoshi began serialization on the Comic Gardo website in July 2019. The excitement continues to grow as an anime television series adaptation, produced by Seven Arcs, is scheduled to premiere in 2024.

In this article, we aim to provide you with comprehensive information about the Failure Frame Anime. By delving into the details presented here, you can gain a deeper understanding of what to expect from this intriguing series.

Is It Confirmed that The Anime Will Failure Frame Is Happening?

A new trailer for the anime series called Failure Frame: I Became the Strongest and Annihilated Everything with Low-Level Spells has been released, confirming its release later this year. The trailer also revealed a key visual featuring the main character, Mimori Touka, suffering in the dungeon where the story begins.

The trailer provided details about the team behind the project. Director Michio Fukuda, scriptwriter Yasuhiro Nakanishi, LO supervision by Kenji Masuda, prop design by Goichi Iwahata, world setting by Kio Edamtsu, sound direction by Nobuyuki Abe, and art direction by Yoko Nakao are among the key staff members involved.

The animation will be handled by Studio Seven Arcs, but a specific release date hasn’t been confirmed yet. However, it is assumed that the series will be launched in 2024.

What Is the Plot of Failure Frame Anime?

Failure Frame may appear to be a sci-fi animated show initially, but its complete title reveals its true genre.

The official title is “Failure Frame: I Became the Strongest and Annihilated Everything with Low-Level Spells. This series falls under the isekai anime category and can also be described as a dark fantasy due to its more serious and ominous approach to the genre.

The plot centers around Mimori Touka, who, along with his classmates, gets transported to a fantasy world. However, things take a grim turn when the goddess who summoned them banishes Mimori to a dungeon, deeming him too weak to be a hero.

Despite having only an E-rank, Mimori discovers he possesses unique abilities. Fueled by this revelation, he makes a vow to seek revenge against the goddess who brought them to this world.
